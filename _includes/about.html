The Band

Formed in 2010, Playing for Kicks have taken our eclectic assortment of rock and pop covers as far afield as France and Wales, but mainly all corners of Bradford. Our line-up has changed little over more than 140 gigs but we're back up to a five piece for 2023.

Mike

Guitar/Vocals

Plays the easy bits and leaves the tricky stuff to others, mainly so he can bop about, while imagining he’s in front of the mirror with a hairbrush.

Eddie

Bass

Somehow combines laid back cool with great dynamic playing. Joined us in 2016 and has proved a real asset!

Cath

Drums/Vocals

Jonathan
